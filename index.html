What is OpenCryptoPay?
With OpenCryptoPay, a merchant enables its customers to pay for purchases with digital Swiss francs on the blockchain, the Eurocoin, or with other digital currencies such as Bitcoin, USDT, or other currencies.
Learn more
How does it work?
OpenCryptoPay does not require a license for payments. In order to participate, you only need to comply with the technical standards for OpenCryptoPay. Merchants may still need a financial intermediary license to use or offer this system as a service, depending on the laws of their country.
‍
The OpenCryptoPay Ecosystem has service providers ready to offer their services as financial intermediaries. This means that the merchant has nothing to do with the payment itself and receives the purchase amount conveniently in their bank account.
