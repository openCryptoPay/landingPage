Free & Open Payment Network.
Open CryptoPay is an open license-free P2P payment standard for all blockchains and crypto assets and is also Bitcoin Lightning LNURL backward compatible.
Easily accept digital assets.
With OpenCryptoPay, you don't need blockchain software or special devices to accept digital assets. Use your existing contactless terminal hardware (NFC) or the OpenCryptoPay app to accept payments.

What is OpenCryptoPay?
With OpenCryptoPay, a merchant enables its customers to pay for purchases with digital Swiss francs on the blockchain, the Eurocoin, or with other digital currencies such as Bitcoin, USDT, or other currencies.
Learn more
How to pay using Open CryptoPay as a customer.
As a customer, you can easily pay for your purchases directly from your pocket and your favourite wallet. To do this, simply scan the shop's QR code and the payment will be initiated. You can decide and choose which of your stablecoins or cryptocurrencies you want to use for the purchase. The merchant receives the order amount either in the desired currency, e.g. CHF or EUR, transferred to his bank account, or he can choose to receive it in cryptocurrency.

How to accept payments as a merchant.
To accept payments at your existing checkout system, it is often sufficient to place a dedicated QR code in the checkout area. At the end of the checkout process, the cashier selects Open CryptoPay, the shopper scans the QR code with their wallet app and confirms the transaction, and that's it. The payment process is very simple and takes only a few seconds.
